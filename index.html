Profile

Biomedical AI researcher and educator

May Phu Paing is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at King Mongkut's Institute of Technology Ladkrabang, Bangkok, Thailand. Her research focuses on computer-aided diagnosis using medical images, with strong experience in image processing, machine learning, deep learning, segmentation, classification, and explainable AI for healthcare.
